Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Welcome to MovieNest ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (movienest.app) and our mobile/desktop applications.

1. Information We Collect

MovieNest is designed to provide catalog and streaming playback services with maximum privacy. We collect minimal information required to run the service:

  • Authentication Information: If you register or log in, we use Firebase Authentication to manage your account credentials securely.
  • Technical Connection Logs: Like standard web platforms, our servers log connection requests to prevent abuse. These logs contain IP addresses but are not linked to any personal identity.
  • Application Settings: Preferences such as language and player resolution are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information strictly to:

  • Provide and maintain the MovieNest streaming services.
  • Authenticate user access and secure accounts.
  • Monitor and prevent technical issues or service abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services that process data securely under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure user authentication and database management.
  • Vercel: Used to host our web application services.

4. Data Storage and Security

All connection data in transit is encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3 (HTTPS) security. Your data is stored on secure Google Cloud Firebase database servers. We implement administrative and technical controls to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

5. No Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share user personal data with any third-party marketing, advertising, or analytics networks.
